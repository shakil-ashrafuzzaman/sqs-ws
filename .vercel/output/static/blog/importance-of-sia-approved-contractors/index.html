The Importance of SIA Approved Contractors

SQS Security Expert
1 July 2026
5 min read

When securing your business, cutting corners is never an option. Partnering with an SIA (Security Industry Authority) Approved Contractor guarantees that your security provider meets the highest standards of professionalism and compliance. SQS Security is proud to hold this accreditation, ensuring our operatives are fully vetted, extensively trained, and legally compliant.

An Approved Contractor Status (ACS) is only awarded to companies that undergo rigorous independent assessments. By choosing an ACS accredited firm, you are protecting your assets, your reputation, and most importantly, your people.
