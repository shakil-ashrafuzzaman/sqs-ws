Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from FM companies, project managers, and property teams.

Are all your security guards SIA licensed?
Yes. Every security officer we deploy holds a current SIA (Security Industry Authority) licence. This is a legal requirement in the UK and ensures our personnel have passed rigorous identity checks, criminal record checks, and relevant security training.
Do you provide emergency or short-notice cover?
Absolutely. We understand that security gaps happen without warning. SQS Security responds quickly to urgent cover requests, providing SIA-licensed officers without complicated procurement delays.
What kind of reporting do you provide?
We provide clear, written reporting for every deployment. Shift reports, access logs, and incident records are delivered to clients after every assignment, so you never have to chase us for documentation.
Do you cover my specific area?
While our headquarters is based in Southampton, we provide comprehensive, UK-wide deployment. Whether you are in London, Manchester, Brighton, or Birmingham, we have local experts ready to secure your premises.
